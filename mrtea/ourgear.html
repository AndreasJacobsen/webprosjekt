Tea a la Boriz!

To make tea like Boris, you will need the following tools and ingredienses:

  • Your favorite teacup
  • Tea filter
  • Tea-spoon
  • Tea (not in bags, but loose-leaf)
  • Something to boile water in (boiler, kettle)
  • Clean cold water.
  • Somewhere to put your used filter (a small bowl)

Here's how it's done:

  1. Boil the water for your favorite teacup
  2. As we wait for the water to boil, find your favorite tea, a teaspoon and a filter.
  3. Use your spoon to fill the filter half-full with tea. Do not fill it all the way, because the tea expands inside the filter, to almost double it's size.
  4. Put your filter inside your favorite cup and pour your boiling water over the teabag. It is important to have boiling hot water, or there will be a mad english man at your door arguing about your not-boiling “dishwashingwater”. No boiling water in hot tea, is a sin.
  5. Let your tea sit for atleast 3-5 minutes, not more cause the antioxidants will produce a bitter taste witch is not plessant.
  6. Milk, suggar and sorcery does not belong in tea. It shall be enjoyed bare. The only thing there is to it is if your have let your antioxidants set, add a few drops of lemon and your all done.
  7. Enjoy your tea a la Boriz!
